About

My name is Steven Bopp and I am a Ph.D. student and NDSEG fellow at the Universty of California, San Diego studying materials science and engineering (one of my absolute passions in life). I work with the Zhaowei Liu group here at UCSD, and several collaborators elsewhere including my brother Douglas at NIST, Boulder. My research focuses on the development of a diverse platform of quantum-engineered, nitride-based, subwavelength nanocomposites with extreme nonlinearities to bring practical nonlinear optics to chip scale optoelectronics but my research interests extend into broad categories of photonic, electronic, plasmonic, and bio materials .
